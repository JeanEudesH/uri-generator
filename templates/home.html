Welcome to PHIS URI generator, This tool is meant to explore the different URI generated by PHIS.

You can use it to create your own URIs but it lacks customization at the moment.

The generator proceeds by concatenating character strings so you may generate two identicals URI if you enter the same values in the fields.

To give it a try, please select a type of object among the different categories proposed. Important!

You might very well have already identifiers with suitable complexity and fullfiling the requirements of your own system.
In order to be plainly interoperable with other information system in the EPPN2020 network, what was proposed is to prefix your existing identifiers.
